Project Details

Text to sense

How A.I. Can Convert Text to Sense


A.I offers solutions to transform text into multi-sensory experiences, enhancing how we perceive and interact with written content. By leveraging multiple models, text can be converted into immersive and customisable sensory outputs.

By transforming text into a holistic sensory experience, literature can cater to more diverse learning styles and preferences. This innovation not only enhances storytelling but also makes content more accessible and engaging, offering a unique way to experience written words.

Concept Demonstration

Mechanism Behind the Demo Concept


1. Input: Text is inserted, or an extract from chosen literature is generated by GPT-4o.

2. Speech: Elevenlabs or OpenAI TTS models convert text into narration.

3. Vision: The text is translated by GPT-4o into a prompt for DALL-E to visualise.

4. Music: GPT-4o requests an instrumental of the user-chosen genre from the Spotify API to complement the mood and context.

5. Output: A combination of vision and audio converts text into a multi-sensory output.

Enhanced audio books

Project Aim

Sensescribe aims to ehance the experience of literature by integrating live imagery and music tailored to both the contextual narrative, and user preferences.

The service will combine the auditory immersion of traditional audiobooks with dynamic visual and musical elements to enrich the experience, and cater to diverse learning styles and preferences.

The project aims to transcend limitations of text to advance the bandwith of the communication of information and emotion.
