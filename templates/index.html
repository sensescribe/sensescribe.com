Project Details

Text to sense

How A.I. Can Convert Text to Sense


A.I offers solutions to transform text into multi-sensory experiences, enhancing how we perceive and interact with written content. By leveraging multiple models, text can be converted into immersive and customisable sensory outputs.

By combining various models and databases, text can be transformed into a holistic sensory experience, catering to diverse learning styles and preferences. This innovation not only enhances storytelling but also makes content more accessible and engaging, offering a unique way to experience written words.

Concept Demonstration

Mechanism Behind the Demo Concept


1. Text: The user inputs text.

2. Speech: OpenAI TTS converts text into natural and expressive narration.

3. Vision: Text processed by GPT-3.5 generates prompt for DALL-E to visualise.

4. Music: GPT-4o requests a fitting instrumental from the Spotify API to complement the mood and context.

5. Output: Combination of vision and audio converts text to multi-sensory experience.

Enhanced audio books

Project Aim

Sensescribe aims to revolutionise the experience of literature by integrating live imagery and music tailored to both the narrative, and user preferences.

The service will combine the auditory immersion of traditional audiobooks with dynamic visual and musical elements to not only enrich the storytelling experience, but to also cater to diverse learning styles and preferences.

the project aims to transcend limitations of text to advance the bandwith of the communication of information and emotion in a way that is comparable to the evolution of text itself.
